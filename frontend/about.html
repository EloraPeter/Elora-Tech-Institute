| Our Story |

Elora Tech Institute was founded to bridge the gap between aspiring tech talents and real-world skills. We believe that everyone — from young children to adults — deserves the opportunity to build a successful tech career, no matter their starting point.

| Our Mission & Vision |

Our Mission

Empower individuals with cutting-edge technology skills and the confidence to innovate, lead, and transform the world.

Our Vision

A future where African talents lead global innovation, with ETI as a world-class institute nurturing tech leaders.

| Our Values |

Innovation: Embracing creativity.
Excellence: Achieving high standards.
Community: Growing together.
Integrity: Leading with honesty.
Empowerment: Opportunities for all.

| Our Future Plans |

We are excited to expand beyond online learning with our first physical campus, accommodating:

  • Children (Crèche to SS3) for early tech education
  • Industrial Training students for hands-on experience
  • Adults eager to gain in-demand tech skills

ETI is committed to opening branches across Nigeria and internationally.
