      | Our Story |

      Elora Tech Institute (ETI) was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves access to quality tech education, no matter their background or age. What started as a virtual coding class has grown into a full-fledged institute dedicated to raising Africa’s next generation of innovators, developers, and digital leaders. Founded by passionate educators and tech professionals, ETI bridges the gap between learning and opportunity. We train students from crèche to SS3, undergraduates, and postgraduates, offering hands-on programs in web development, cybersecurity, robotics, AI, IoT, and more. With a vision to expand across Nigeria and beyond, ETI is not just a school — it’s a movement. A place where dreams are built, skills are sharpened, and futures are transformed. We’re not just teaching tech. We’re building legacies.

      | Our Future Plans |

      At Elora Tech Institute (ETI), we are proud to be expanding beyond the boundaries of online learning with the establishment of our first physical campus — a major milestone in our mission to make quality tech education accessible to everyone.

      This campus is purposefully designed to serve a diverse range of learners, including:

      • Children (Crèche to SS3): Introducing young minds to the world of technology early, fostering creativity, curiosity, and confidence in digital skills from their foundational years.
      • Industrial Training Students: Providing university and polytechnic students with practical, hands-on experience in real-world tech environments to bridge the gap between classroom theory and industry expectations.
      • Adults and Professionals: Equipping career changers, job seekers, and professionals with up-to-date, in-demand tech skills that open doors to new opportunities in today’s evolving digital economy.

      Our vision doesn't stop there. ETI is committed to scaling our impact by establishing branches across Nigeria and internationally. We aim to become a leading hub for tech education in Africa and beyond — nurturing talent, inspiring innovation, and building the digital leaders of tomorrow.

We are committed to providing inclusive, practical, and high-quality education that inspires confidence, nurtures creativity, and develops innovative thinkers equipped to lead and drive meaningful transformation in their communities and beyond.
