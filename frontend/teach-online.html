      | Frequently Asked Questions |

      Who can become a tutor on ETI?

      Anyone with valuable skills and a passion for teaching can become a tutor on ETI. Whether you're a professional, freelancer, or enthusiast, your expertise is welcome.

      Do I need prior teaching experience?

      No prior teaching experience is required. ETI provides guidance and tools to help you structure your lessons, so you can focus on sharing your knowledge with confidence.

      How do I get paid as a tutor?

      Tutors earn income for every student signup. Earnings are sent directly to your preferred payment method on a regular schedule.

      What support does ETI provide for new tutors?

      We offer starter guides, planning tools, a Tutor Hub, and dedicated Tutor Assistance Crew to help you through every step — from creating your first tutorial to engaging with students.

      Can I teach in my own language?

      Yes! ETI supports over 60 languages, allowing you to connect with learners in your native or preferred language.
