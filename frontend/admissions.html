Welcome to ETI Admissions

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is there an age limit?

No, ETI is open to children, teenagers, young adults, and professionals.

Can I join if I'm outside Nigeria?

Yes! We are building an international structure. Our online programs are available worldwide.

Do I need a tech background?

No prior experience needed. We teach you from beginner to expert!

Elora Tech Institute

Elora Tech Institute equips individuals with in-demand tech skills through hands-on training in web development, cybersecurity, Web3, IoT, and more. Our vision is to be a leading tech institution, shaping the future of tech education through innovation and excellence.
