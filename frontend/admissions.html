      | Frequently Asked Questions |

      Is there an age limit to enroll at ETI?

      Absolutely not! ETI warmly welcomes learners of all ages—from young children starting their first steps in coding, to teenagers exploring tech fields, to young adults and seasoned professionals seeking to upgrade their skills. Our programs are carefully designed to cater to varying learning needs and levels, ensuring that everyone has an opportunity to thrive in technology.

      Can I join ETI if I live outside Nigeria?

      Definitely! Our online learning platform is accessible globally, allowing students from any country to enroll and benefit from our expertly crafted courses. While we currently offer robust online programs, we are excited to soon introduce international physical campuses and hybrid learning options to serve a wider community worldwide.

      Do I need any prior tech background to join ETI?

      No prior experience or technical background is required! Our curriculum is designed to take you from absolute beginner to expert level through hands-on learning, practical projects, and guided mentorship. Whether you’re starting fresh or looking to sharpen your existing skills, ETI supports your growth every step of the way.

      What are the admission requirements for ETI’s physical campus?

      Admission to our physical campus requires completion of the application form, submission of academic transcripts or certificates relevant to your desired program, and an entrance interview or assessment to gauge your readiness. Additionally, for younger students (crèche to SS3), parental consent and guardianship details are required. Our team is happy to guide you through each step to make your enrollment smooth.

      How do I apply for online courses at ETI?

      Applying for online courses is simple! Just visit our website, select the course or certification you’re interested in, and complete the online registration form. Once your registration is confirmed, you will receive login credentials to access the learning platform immediately. Support is available if you need help with course selection or technical setup.

      Are there scholarships or financial aid options for ETI students?

      Yes! ETI is committed to making technology education accessible. We offer a variety of scholarships, grants, and flexible payment plans for both physical and online students. Eligibility criteria and application details are available on our website. We encourage prospective students to apply early to take advantage of these opportunities.
