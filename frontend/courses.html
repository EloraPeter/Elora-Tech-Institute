      | Flexible Learning Options |

      We understand that everyone learns differently. That’s why our programs are designed with flexibility in mind so you can study at your own pace, on your own schedule, and in a way that aligns with your lifestyle and long-term goals.

      Online Learning

      Join classes from anywhere in the world through our robust online platform. You’ll have access to live instructor-led sessions, recorded replays, downloadable resources, and interactive assignments—allowing you to learn on your terms without compromising quality.

      In-Person Classes

      Our upcoming Delta State campus will offer immersive, hands-on classroom experiences. Engage in collaborative projects, access on-site mentorship, and benefit from real-world labs designed to give you practical, job-ready skills.

      Hybrid Model

      Prefer a mix of both? Our hybrid option combines the convenience of online learning with the structure of in-person support. Attend virtual lectures and supplement them with physical workshops, bootcamps, and campus activities for a truly personalized approach.

      | Frequently Asked Questions |

      What are the prerequisites for enrolling?

      Most of our beginner-level courses require no prior tech experience. We designed them to be friendly to absolute beginners, guiding you through foundational concepts step-by-step. However, intermediate and advanced tracks may require basic familiarity with tools like HTML, Git, or general computer usage. For each course, you'll find a clear outline of prerequisites to help you choose the right level.

      How long are the programs?

      Our program durations vary depending on the course and intensity. Short courses typically last 6 to 8 weeks, perfect for focused skill development. Full tracks or diplomas can range from 3 to 12 months and are designed to help you transition into the tech industry or upskill significantly. All courses are structured to be flexible, allowing you to learn at your own pace with both live and recorded sessions.

      Are there payment plans available?

      Absolutely. We understand that finances can be a challenge, especially for students. That’s why we offer flexible payment plans, including monthly or bi-weekly installments. We also offer early bird discounts, group enrollment discounts, and limited scholarships for students with financial needs. To explore payment options, please reach out to our support team or check the course page.

      Do you offer certifications?

      Yes. Every student who completes a course or program will receive a digital certificate of completion that is verifiable and shareable on LinkedIn. In addition, many of our programs are designed to prepare you for globally recognized certifications such as AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, CompTIA A+, Google IT Support, and more. We also offer guidance and practice exams to help you prepare effectively.

      Can I learn while working or schooling full-time?

      Yes, our programs are designed with flexibility in mind. You can access live classes in the evenings or weekends, and we record every session so you can catch up anytime. You’ll also have lifetime access to course content and a self-paced study option if live sessions don't work for you. Whether you’re a full-time student or working professional, you can learn comfortably on your schedule.

      Is there mentorship or support during the course?

      Yes. Every student gets access to a dedicated mentor or course facilitator who provides weekly check-ins, project feedback, and career guidance. We also have active community support groups on WhatsApp and Discord, where you can ask questions, share wins, and connect with fellow learners. You're never alone at ETI — we support you from start to finish.

      What kind of projects will I work on?

      You’ll work on real-world projects that reflect current industry standards. For example, web development students might build responsive websites, portfolios, and client dashboards. Mobile app students might build a complete app prototype with user authentication. You'll also participate in capstone projects and hackathons, giving you strong, portfolio-ready work to showcase to future employers or clients.
